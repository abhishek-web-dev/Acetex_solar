High Mast Solar Street Lights

Acetex Energy’s High Mast Solar Street Lights offer powerful, eco-friendly LED illumination for large-scale applications such as highways, stadiums, and industrial zones. These lights combine high-efficiency solar panels with robust LED technology, delivering bright, uniform lighting without reliance on the electrical grid.

Designed for durability and low maintenance, Acetex High Mast Solar Street Lights feature advanced lithium-ion batteries and monocrystalline solar panels, ensuring reliable performance even in challenging conditions. With a lifespan of up to 50,000 hours for the LEDs, these lights provide sustainable, cost-effective lighting for expansive areas, aligning with Acetex Energy’s commitment to green solutions.

Features and Advantages

Acetex High Mast Solar Street Lights are engineered for exceptional performance, featuring high-capacity monocrystalline solar panels and powerful LED modules that deliver up to 20,000 lumens for wide-area coverage. The elevated design ensures optimal light distribution over large spaces, making them ideal for high-traffic and critical infrastructure areas.

Equipped with intelligent control systems, including dusk-to-dawn sensors and optional motion detection, these lights optimize energy usage by adjusting brightness based on environmental conditions. The IP65 waterproof and corrosion-resistant construction ensures durability in harsh climates, while the modular design simplifies maintenance and upgrades.

Additional features include a high color rendering index (CRI) for enhanced visibility and safety, and advanced thermal management to ensure long-term reliability. By choosing Acetex High Mast Solar Street Lights, municipalities and industries can achieve sustainable, high-performance lighting with minimal environmental impact and reduced operational costs.

Applications and Use Cases

Acetex Energy’s High Mast Solar Street Lights are designed for large-scale applications, including highways, airports, stadiums, and industrial complexes. Their high-lumen output and solar-powered design provide reliable, eco-friendly illumination for expansive areas without the need for grid connectivity.

These lights are ideal for illuminating major roadways, intersections, and public spaces, ensuring safety and visibility for vehicles and pedestrians. In industrial settings, such as warehouses and manufacturing plants, they provide consistent lighting for operational efficiency and security. The grid-independent design makes them suitable for remote or off-grid locations.

With smart control features, including remote monitoring and adjustable brightness, Acetex High Mast Solar Street Lights offer flexibility for large-scale infrastructure projects. Their robust construction and high-efficiency components make them a sustainable choice for municipalities, transportation authorities, and commercial facilities.
