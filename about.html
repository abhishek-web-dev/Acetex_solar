About Acetex Solar Energy

At Acetex Solar Energy, we are passionate about creating a brighter and greener tomorrow. Our solutions combine innovation, sustainability, and reliability to redefine modern lighting and energy systems.

Company's Journey

Established in 2020, Acetex Solar Energy began with a mission to revolutionize lighting and energy solutions in India and beyond. From a small unit focusing on LED street lights, we have expanded into a full-fledged manufacturer of solar-powered innovations.

Our journey has been marked by continuous R&D, strong industry collaborations, and a commitment to delivering sustainable alternatives to conventional energy systems. Today, our products illuminate highways, urban streets, industries, schools, and homes across multiple states.

Purpose and Goals

We believe energy is the foundation of progress. Our purpose is to deliver reliable, eco-friendly lighting and energy solutions that not only serve customers but also contribute to a cleaner planet. Our goals for the next decade are clear and ambitious:

  • Sustainability Leadership: Develop 100% renewable product lines, reducing carbon footprint globally.
  • Innovation Excellence: Launch cutting-edge LED and solar technologies every year.
  • Quality Assurance: Maintain international certifications and rigorous quality testing.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Provide after-sales support and long-term warranties.
  • Market Expansion: Grow our presence in both domestic and international markets.

Introduction to the Team

At the heart of Acetex Solar Energy is a team of visionaries, engineers, and innovators who believe in the power of sustainable energy. Guided by Mr. Divyanshu Srivastav, our leadership emphasizes ethics, quality, and customer trust.

Our R&D specialists work tirelessly to design energy-efficient products, while our manufacturing experts ensure flawless execution. With a strong customer service team, we provide ongoing support, making us more than just a supplier — we are partners in progress.

Our Offerings

Our portfolio is designed to meet diverse needs, ranging from public infrastructure to residential and industrial applications. Every product is crafted with modern technology, ensuring energy savings and superior performance.
