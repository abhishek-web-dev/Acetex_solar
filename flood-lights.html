LED Flood Lights

Acetex Energy’s LED Flood Lights are engineered to provide powerful, high-intensity illumination for a variety of outdoor and indoor applications. Utilizing advanced LED technology, these lights deliver exceptional brightness and energy efficiency, making them ideal for stadiums, parking lots, warehouses, and security lighting. Our flood lights ensure reliable performance and significant cost savings.

Constructed with durable, weather-resistant materials, Acetex LED Flood Lights are designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions while maintaining consistent performance. With a lifespan of up to 60,000 hours, these lights minimize maintenance costs and offer long-term reliability. Acetex Energy’s commitment to quality ensures our flood lights meet global standards, making them a preferred choice for high-performance lighting solutions.

Features and Advantages

Acetex LED Flood Lights are designed for superior performance, offering high-lumen output for expansive and uniform illumination. The advanced LED modules reduce energy consumption by up to 70% compared to traditional flood lighting, lowering electricity costs. Their robust construction, with IP65/IP66 waterproofing and corrosion-resistant materials, ensures durability in extreme conditions, from heavy rain to high temperatures.

  • Input Voltage Range: 145 VAC-270 VAC
  • LED Luminous Density: 120-150 LM/Watt
  • Color Temperature: 2700K-6500K
  • Working Temperature: -25°C to +70°C
  • Storage Temperature: -40°C to +70°C
  • Housing: Pressure die cast aluminium alloy
  • Secondary Optics: Suitable optical lens
  • Rated LED Life Span: 60,000 hours
  • Color Rendering Index: 80
  • Power Factor: > 0.9
  • Efficiency: > 85%
  • IP Grade: IP65/IP66

These lights feature adjustable mounting brackets for flexible installation and beam angle customization, making them suitable for diverse applications. Smart control options, including dimming and motion sensors, allow for optimized energy usage and enhanced security. The sleek, modern design of Acetex LED Flood Lights complements any environment while delivering exceptional lighting performance.

Applications and Use Cases

Acetex Energy’s LED Flood Lights are versatile solutions designed for a wide range of applications, including sports arenas, parking lots, industrial facilities, and security lighting. Their high-lumen output and wide beam angles ensure bright, uniform illumination, enhancing safety and visibility in large outdoor and indoor spaces.

In commercial settings, these flood lights are ideal for illuminating building facades, billboards, and parking areas, providing enhanced security and aesthetic appeal. For industrial applications, such as warehouses and construction sites, they offer reliable lighting for safe operations. Sports facilities benefit from their powerful illumination, ensuring clear visibility for players and spectators.

Acetex LED Flood Lights are also used for security purposes, with motion sensor compatibility to deter intruders and reduce energy consumption. Their durable design and smart control integration make them a cost-effective choice for municipalities, businesses, and institutions looking to enhance safety and efficiency in large-scale lighting projects.
